About Us

Cliff Skillings, a proud resident of Ketchikan, Alaska, is the passionate owner and driving force behind Eddystone Rock and Ready Mix. With deep roots in the local community, Cliff has dedicated himself to serving the construction needs of Ketchikan and its surrounding areas.

Cliff's journey in the concrete industry began years ago when he recognized the demand for reliable concrete solutions tailored to Alaska's unique environment. Drawing from his extensive knowledge and experience, Cliff founded Eddystone Rock and Ready Mix with a vision to provide top-notch concrete products and exceptional service to his fellow community members.

As a hands-on owner, Cliff is actively involved in every aspect of the business, from overseeing operations to ensuring customer satisfaction. His leadership is guided by a commitment to integrity, honesty, and excellence in all endeavors.

Beyond his role in the business, Cliff is deeply invested in the well-being of Ketchikan and its residents. He actively participates in community initiatives and supports local causes, demonstrating his dedication to giving back to the place he calls home.
