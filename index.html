Welcome to Eddystone Rock and Ready Mix

At Eddystone Rock and Ready Mix, we take pride in being a leading provider of concrete solutions in Ketchikan, Alaska, and surrounding areas. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted source for high-quality concrete products and reliable services.

Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do. We understand the unique challenges of construction projects in Alaska's rugged environment, and we tailor our products and services to meet the specific needs of our customers. Whether you're working on residential, commercial, or industrial projects, you can rely on us to deliver durable and dependable concrete solutions.

What sets us apart is our dedication to customer satisfaction. From the moment you contact us to the completion of your project, our team is here to support you every step of the way. We prioritize open communication, timely delivery, and superior craftsmanship to ensure your project's success.

As a locally-owned and operated company, we are deeply rooted in the Ketchikan community. We take pride in contributing to the growth and development of our region through our work. Our team consists of skilled professionals who are passionate about what they do and are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and safety.

Whether you need ready-mix concrete, custom concrete mixes, or concrete pumping services, Eddystone Rock and Ready Mix is your trusted partner for all your concrete needs in Ketchikan, Alaska. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you bring your vision to life.
